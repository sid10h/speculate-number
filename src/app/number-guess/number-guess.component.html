How to Play

1. The aim of the game is to correctly the guess the three digit number.
2. The player can make ten guesses. Corresponding to each guess there will be a message.
3. The message will indicate the number of correct digits guessed.
4. 'CNCP' indicates a digit that is correct and in its right place.
5. 'CN' indicates a digit that is correct but not in its correct place.
